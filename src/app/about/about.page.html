About Welcome Game

Welcome Game is an interactive platform designed to provide a fun and engaging experience for foreign students at UTBM. Whether you're exploring maps, completing challenges, or competing in events, our goal is to create a vibrant community where players can connect, learn, and grow together.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring all students together through immersive games and events. We believe that gaming is more than just entertainment it’s a way to connect with others, develop new skills, and have fun. Welcome Game aims to provide a platform that fosters creativity, collaboration, and friendly competition.

Features

  • Explore maps: Dive into diverse maps with unique challenges and objectives.
  • Connect with friends: Add friends, join games together, and chat while playing.
  • Participate in events: Join exciting events and earn points as you compete.
  • Manage your profile: Customize your profile and keep track of your achievements.
