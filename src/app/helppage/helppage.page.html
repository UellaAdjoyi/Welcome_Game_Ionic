Help Welcome Game

Welcome to the Welcome Game! Here's how to make the most out of your experience:

Getting Started

When you first enter the app, you'll be able to explore various maps, connect with friends, and participate in exciting events. Each map offers unique challenges and rewards, so make sure to explore them all!

Playing the Game

To begin playing, simply select a map from the available options. You can move around the map, explore different areas, and interact with objects. Some maps have specific goals you need to accomplish, so pay attention to the objectives!

Connecting with Friends

Welcome Game allows you to connect with friends and other players. You can add friends, join multiplayer games, and communicate via chat. To add a friend, simply search for their username and send them a request.

Managing Events

If you're an administrator, you'll have the ability to create and manage events. Events are a great way to bring players together for fun and competition. To create an event, go to the 'Manage Events' section and follow the instructions.

FAQ

  • How do I change my profile information? Go to your profile page to update your name, avatar, and other details.
  • How do I earn points? Points are earned by completing tasks, participating in events, and achieving milestones. The more you play, the more points you earn!
  • What if I need help? If you're having trouble, feel free to contact our support team through the app's settings page.
